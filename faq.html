Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

A quick reference for delivery formats, production timelines, revisions, permits, and studio bookings. If your question is not covered here, reach out and we will answer directly.

Book a Session Contact Us
What formats do you deliver?↓
We deliver in all standard formats: ProRes, DNxHD, H.264, and more. Raw footage is available upon request.
How long does production take?↓
Timeline varies by project scope. Typical turnaround is 2-4 weeks from shoot to final delivery, with rush options available when schedule allows.
Do you offer color grading?↓
Yes. All projects include professional color grading and sound design. VFX and animation can be added separately.
Can we book the CYC wall for just a few hours?↓
Yes, we offer flexible hourly booking. Minimum 1-hour sessions are available, with package discounts for full-day rentals.
Do you handle licensing and permits?↓
We manage necessary permits for location shoots. Clients remain responsible for music licensing unless otherwise arranged.
What's your revision policy?↓
We include 2 rounds of revisions in standard packages. Additional revisions are billed at $150/hour.
