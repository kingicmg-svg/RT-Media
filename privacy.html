Privacy Policy

Round Table Media respects your privacy. This page outlines what information we collect through booking and inquiry forms, and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email, and optional phone number provided through website forms.
  • Project details submitted in inquiry or booking messages.
  • Operational metadata required to process bookings and confirmations.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to booking requests and production inquiries.
  • To send confirmations, receipts, and schedule updates.
  • To operate and improve studio booking operations.

Data Retention

We keep submitted information only as long as necessary for business and legal purposes.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact bookings@roundtablemedia.ca.
